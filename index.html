How to use the MOU e-Sign app

Part 1 — #TEACH staff: prepare & send
1

Fill in the program details

Enter the State and Effective date, then the five figures in the Pricing schedule. The document preview on the right updates live as you type.

2

Add who it goes to

Under Send to district, put your name and email (you’ll receive the signed copy), then the district recipient’s name and email. If your site uses a staff access code, enter it here.

3

Send for signature

Click Send for Signature. The district is emailed a private signing link, and you get a confirmation email with a link to track status. Your #TEACH President/Founder signature is already applied — you don’t sign again.

Part 2 — The district: review & sign
4

Open the emailed link

The district clicks Review & Sign the MOU in their email. The program details you set appear as read-only; they can’t change them.

5

Complete district info & sign

They fill in the district name, address, and signer name/title, then draw or type a signature and check the consent box.

6

Done — everyone gets a copy

On Sign & Send Signed Copy, a fully executed PDF is emailed automatically to both the district and #TEACH, and the record is saved.

Checking status
✓

Track any agreement

Use the status link in your confirmation email to see whether the district has signed yet.

Good to know

  • The Send button needs both your email and the district’s email before it will submit.
  • Signing links are private but not password-protected — anyone with the link can sign, so send to the correct address.
  • An MOU can only be signed once; after that the link shows “already signed.”
  • To prepare another agreement, finish the current one and click Prepare another MOU.
